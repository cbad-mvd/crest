Admission to CREST

Students must be referred to CREST by their local school district in order to enroll, and a contract between the local school district and CREST will set forth the specific terms and conditions pursuant to which the student is admitted. For more information on beginning the referral process, please contact us.

CREST Education Center, Inc. admits students of any race, color, national origin, and ethnic origin to all the rights, privileges, programs, and activities generally accorded or made available to students at the school. CREST Education Center, Inc. does not discriminate on the basis of race, color, national origin, and ethnic origin in administration of its educational policies, scholarship and loan programs (if any), and athletic and other school-administered programs.
