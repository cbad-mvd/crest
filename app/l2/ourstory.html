The CREST Difference

CREST’s defining difference is that it is not reliant upon a specific approach or modality, but rather utilizes an eclectic approach that allows each student to demonstrate his or her true ability and areas of interest rather than being defined by either diagnosis or symptomology. As each student is unique, the approach to their educational experiences must be equally unique – a hallmark of CREST’s philosophy. In the words of noted educational psychologist O. Ivar Lovaas: “If they can’t learn the way we teach, we teach the way they learn.”
